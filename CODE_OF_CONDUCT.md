# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in ODS-E respectful and harassment-free for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive in feedback.
- Focusing on technical substance, not personal attacks.
- Accepting accountability and correcting mistakes quickly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of conduct.

## Scope

This code applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and related community channels.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to:

- support@asoba.co

Maintainers will review reports promptly and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, or contributions that violate this code.
